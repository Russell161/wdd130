Content

Home page

This page will be for fans of Ohio State football specifically. The main page will consist of general pictures and highlights of Ohio State football. Those who visit my site will be looking to see some pictures and general information about important moments in the history of Ohio State football as I remember. The important games and moments that will be highlighted will include records being set and memorable moments from important games.

Images for the Home page

Ohio State vs Alabama

This page will be completely focused on a specific important game in the history of Ohio State football. This semi final game took place in the first ever College Football Playoff between powerhouse Alabama and blueblood Ohio State. Many believed that Ohio State did not deserve to be in the playoff and would get destroyed by Alabama. This all time classic ended in an Ohio State win with one of the most iconic plays in the history of the game cementing the victory for Ohio State and catapulting them into the National Championship game that they would eventually win.

"The Game" 2016

The biggest rivalry in college sports known simply as “The Game” takes place every year between perennial powerhouses Ohio State and Michigan. The intensity of this rivalry cannot be captured with mere words, it has to be experienced. This year was no different as both teams were ranked in the top 5. Points that were hard to come by, an iconic stadium with iconic teams in it, an unforgettable play call and controversy marked this remarkable installment of “The Game”.
